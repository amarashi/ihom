Persian miniature (negārgari-ye Irāni) is a highly detailed, small-scale painting style from modern-day Iran. Historically used to illustrate literary manuscripts like the Shahnameh (Book of Kings), these works are famous for their vibrant mineral-based pigments, gold-leaf accents, and a distinct lack of Western-style perspective.
